## Overview
**MT5 (MetaTrader 5)** is the latest generation of the world’s most popular trading platform. It offers advanced charting tools, technical analysis, automated trading with Expert Advisors (EA), and support for all major financial markets.

> [!Important]
> Trading involves significant risk of loss. This software is provided for informational and technical purposes only. Always trade responsibly and never risk more than you can afford to lose.

## Key Features
- Advanced multi-timeframe charting
- Built-in technical indicators and drawing tools
- Expert Advisors and algorithmic trading
- MQL5 programming language support
- Economic calendar and market news
- Multi-asset trading (Forex, Stocks, Futures, CFDs)
- High performance and stability
